    Our History

    Founded to raise disciplined, knowledgeable, and God-fearing young leaders, the school has grown into one of the most reputable institutions in the region.
    Divine Step Schools was established in 1987 as God’swill Day Nursery School, Aba by her founder Mr. Christian Agaodichinma Igwe (TLP) and her Proprietress/first headmistress Ezinne (Mrs) Florence Nnenna U.C. Igwe B.Ed (Obi di Ebube). Because of what the school stands for; care, discipline, academics excellence and perfect moral balance, it grew in leaps and bounds under acclaimed accolades and testimonies that its reformation for expansion became inevitable.
